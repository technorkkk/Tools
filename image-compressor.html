How to compress images online?

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking "Select Image"
  2. Adjust the quality slider to your desired compression level
  3. Click "Compress Image" to process your file
  4. Download your compressed image

About Image Compression

Our image compressor reduces file size by optimizing the image data. Higher quality settings preserve more detail but result in larger files. For web use, we recommend quality between 60-80% for a good balance between quality and file size.

All processing happens in your browser - your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.
