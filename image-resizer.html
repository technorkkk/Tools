How to resize images online?

  1. Upload your image file (JPG, PNG, GIF, or WEBP)
  2. Set your desired width and height in pixels
  3. Choose whether to maintain the original aspect ratio
  4. Select your output format and quality (if applicable)
  5. Click "Resize Image" and download your resized image

About Image Resizing

Our image resizer allows you to quickly change the dimensions of your images while maintaining quality. This is useful for:

  • Reducing file size for web optimization
  • Preparing images for social media
  • Creating thumbnails or preview images
  • Adjusting images for specific device requirements

Note: All processing happens in your browser - your images are never uploaded to our servers.
